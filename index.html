Key Insights & Opportunities

Common Pain Points:

  • Long checkout lines are a universal frustration
  • Price concerns are prevalent (visibility of taxes, overall high prices)
  • Store layouts often require inefficient backtracking
  • Product availability issues (out-of-stock items, poor quality produce)
  • Crowded shopping environments

Design Opportunities:

  • Smart Shopping Path: Technology that optimizes shopping routes based on personal shopping lists
  • Real-time Price & Tax Calculator: App or cart-mounted device showing running total with taxes
  • Queue Management: Systems to reduce wait times or make waiting more efficient
  • Inventory Transparency: Real-time updates on product availability and freshness
  • Multi-store Shopping Management: Tools to coordinate shopping across different stores
  • Quick Delivery Integration: Options for emergency or forgotten items
